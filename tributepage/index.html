Selena Quintanilla

1971 - 1995

"With a positive attitude, you can be anything you want to be." -Selena Quintanilla

  • 1971 - Selena was born in Freeport, TX to Latino parents.
  • 1977 - Quintanilla Jr.(her father) noticed her musical abilities when she was six years old
  • 1980 - Her father opened up a restaurant and had Selena and her siblings perform often
  • 1984 - Selena gained popularity and recorded her first LP record, "Selena y Los Dinos "
  • 1987 - Rudy Trevino, musician and founder of Tejano music awards, discovered Selena. She won the Female Vocalist of the Year award and for nine consecutive years after
  • 1987-95 - Selena continued to release popular albums: "Selena", "Ven conmigo","Entre a mi mundo", "Amor prohibito", "Dreaming of you"
  • 1994 - Throughout the early 90's, Selena opened her own clothing line and botique. Quintanilla family appointed Yolanda Saldivar as manager of Selenas boutiques. Soon after, Selena signed Yolanda as her registered agent
  • 1995 - Selenas iconic and famous performance was at Astrodome in Houston, TX
  • 1995 - Yolanda embezzled more than $30,000 from fan clubs and Selenas boutique. Selena demanded financial papers from Yolanda, and the singer was murdered by a bullet wound during the confrontation in Corpus Christi, TX
